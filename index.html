Report a fake facebook account

Has somebody been pretending to be you on facebook? It's easy to report them!

Step 1

Go to the page of the fake account. Make sure it's the fake account and not your account.

Step 2

  • Click on the button for more options, indicated by "..."
  • Click "Report"

Step 3

  • Click on "Report this Account"
  • Click on "This timeline is pretending to be me or someone I know"

Step 4

Follow the instructions in the dialog. Facebook will review the account and remove it. Once the fake account page no longer works, it has been removed.

Success

After the fake profile has been removed, it will look like this when you try to visit the fake profile.
